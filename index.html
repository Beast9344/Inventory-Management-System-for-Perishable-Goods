About Us

Who We Are

FreshTrack AS is a leading innovator in inventory management solutions, specializing in systems for perishable goods. Founded on the principle of reducing waste and enhancing operational efficiency, our mission is to empower businesses to maintain optimal freshness and streamline their inventory processes.

Our Vision

To become the global standard for perishable inventory management, ensuring businesses thrive sustainably while reducing food waste.

What We Offer

  • Cutting-edge tools for real-time inventory tracking
  • Smart notifications for shelf-life management
  • Seamless integration with existing ERP systems
  • Comprehensive analytics for data-driven decisions

With FreshTrack AS, businesses can minimize loss, maximize efficiency, and contribute to a more sustainable future.
